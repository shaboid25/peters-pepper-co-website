Our Story

Bringing the heat since 2023

The Beginning

What started as a backyard hobby in our garden after Peter was born has grown into an artisan hot sauce kitchen! Sign up for future Sauce Drops or try one of our regular sauces!

Our Mission

We're committed to sourcing the finest peppers using our own family and friends' gardens. From mild bell peppers to scorching hot unique peppers, we believe in quality over quantity and maintaining the authentic flavors that make each batch special.

Quality Promise

Every sauce that leaves our kitchen meets our strict quality standards. We hand-pick each pepper at peak ripeness and ensure our sauces reach you fresh and flavorful. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of our friends and family, and we're ready to share it with you!

Peter's enthusiasm for his hot sauce is second to nobody!
