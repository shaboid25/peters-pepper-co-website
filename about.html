The Beginning

What started as a backyard hobby in our garden after Peter was born has grown into a mail order artisan hot sauce provider! Sign up for future Sauce Drops or try one of our two regular sauces below!

Our Mission

We're committed to sourcing the finest peppers using our own family and friends gardens. From mild bell peppers to scorching hot unique peppers, we believe in quality over quantity and maintaining the authentic flavors that make each batch special.

Quality Promise

Every sauce that leaves our kitchen meets our strict quality standards. We hand-pick each pepper at peak ripeness and ensure they reach you fresh and flavorful. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of our friends and family, and we're ready to share it with you!
