Our Story

Bringing the heat since 1995

The Beginning

What started as a backyard hobby in 1995 has grown into one of the most respected pepper companies in the region. Peter Martinez began growing peppers in his small garden, experimenting with different varieties and perfecting his craft.

Our Mission

We're committed to growing the finest peppers using sustainable farming practices. From mild bell peppers to scorching hot ghost peppers, we believe in quality over quantity and maintaining the authentic flavors that make each variety special.

Quality Promise

Every pepper that leaves our farm meets our strict quality standards. We hand-pick each pepper at peak ripeness and ensure they reach you fresh and flavorful. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of restaurants, markets, and pepper enthusiasts across the country.
