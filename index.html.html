Our Family Story

What started as a family garden project has grown into something truly special. With Peter as our young spokesperson and Abby as our inspiration, we've turned our backyard pepper harvest into a labor of love.

Every bottle of Peter's Pepper Co. hot sauce tells the story of our family - from seed to sauce, garden to table. We've shared our first four batches with family and friends, and now we're excited to share our passion with you.

Our secret? Fresh peppers from our own soil, family recipes passed down and perfected, and the enthusiasm of two kids who believe that everything tastes better with a little heat!

This isn't just a business - it's a legacy we're building for Peter and Abby, one bottle at a time.
